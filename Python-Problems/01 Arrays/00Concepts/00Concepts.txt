1. Sorting:
    A. QuickSort: Efficient average-case time complexity (O(nlog n))
    B. MergeSort: Stable sort, useful when order matters (O(nlog n))

2. Searching:
    A. Binary Search: Fast search in sorted arrays (O(log n))

3. Two Pointers: In-place manipulation, often for sorted arrays (e.g., removing duplicates)

4. Sliding Window: Subarray problems, finding maximum/minimum within a window
